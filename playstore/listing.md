## Full Description (4000 char max)

Enter the cosmic arena and battle players from around the world in VORTEX — a fast-paced, real-time multiplayer game where you control a cosmic orb in a vast universe.

YOUR MISSION
Absorb cosmic particles and devour smaller players to grow your orb. Outmaneuver bigger opponents, use power-ups strategically, and climb the leaderboard to become the ultimate cosmic entity.

HOW TO PLAY
- Move your orb by dragging on screen
- Absorb particles and players smaller than you to grow
- Avoid players larger than you — they can consume you!
- Use abilities like Boost, Split, and Shield to gain the advantage

ABILITIES
- BOOST: Burst of speed to chase or escape (costs mass)
- SPLIT: Eject mass forward to distract chasers
- SHIELD: Temporary invincibility (15-second cooldown)

POWER-UPS
- Speed Boost — 5 seconds of turbo speed
- Mass Orb — Instant +30 mass
- Magnet — Attract nearby food particles
- Ghost — Pass through other players briefly

FEATURES
- Real-time multiplayer battles with players worldwide
- 12 unique cosmic skins — from Inferno to Galaxy
- Earn coins through gameplay to unlock epic skins
- Live leaderboards — compete for the top spot
- Multiple graphics quality settings
- Minimap for tactical awareness
- Kill feed and death stats
- Smooth, lag-free gameplay

SKINS COLLECTION
Customize your orb with stunning visual effects:
- Nova, Inferno, Phantom, Aurora, Void, Solar Flare, Galaxy, Neon Pink, Toxic, Frost, Prismatic, Shadow
- Each skin has unique colors, trails, glow effects, and particle animations

Play for free, earn coins by scoring kills and climbing the leaderboard. No pay-to-win — skins are cosmetic only!
